# VSS on Jetson Thor

This is our first AI application that runs on Jetson Thor. You can get either MIC-742 or MIC-743 to play with this project. Advantech's VSS on Jetson Thor is based on NVIDIA's VSS [version 2.4.1](https://docs.nvidia.com/vss/2.4.1/). We moved the entire system onto the MIC-AI devices.

## Materials

- MIC-742​-AT/MIC-743-AT + **Jetpack 7.1** ([Download 7.1 BSP for MIC-743](https://supr.link/BJuZn))
